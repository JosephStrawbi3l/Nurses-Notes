Introduction

Welcome to Nurses' Notes, your quick and easy reference guide designed especially for nursing students and new nurses. This guide aims to provide essential nursing knowledge and procedures in a simple, straightforward format; perfect for study, review, and on-the-go reminders.



Purpose of Nurses' Notes

This pocket guide is created to support nursing learners by compiling fundamental nursing care concepts, procedures, terminologies, and ethics all in one place. It helps reinforce key skills and knowledge needed in clinical settings.
